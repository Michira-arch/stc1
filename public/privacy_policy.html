Legal Basis for Processing

Under the Data Protection Act, 2019 (Kenya), we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent: You have given clear consent for us to process your personal data for a specific purpose.
  • Contractual Necessity: Processing is necessary for the performance of a contract to which the data subject is a party.
  • Legal Obligation: Processing is necessary for compliance with a legal obligation to which the controller is subject.
  • Legitimate Interests: Processing is necessary for the purposes of the legitimate interests pursued by the data controller or by a third party.
